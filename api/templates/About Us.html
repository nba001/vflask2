About Us

The Diagnomed GmbH was founded in the stunning cathedral city of Cologne in September 2022. In order to give patients comprehensive access to diagnostics and drugs even in remote places, this company was founded on a pandemic-competent premise. Our mission is to provide all-in one place shop for the most advanced IVDs (Immunoassays and molecular diagnostics), Point-of-Care (POC) rapid testing, and medical devices made in Europe and the United States for both human and veterinary diseases like infectious diseases, cancer, autoimmune diseases, gynecology and others.

Pharmaceutical drugs are also offered with the intention of giving disease treatment priority.

We are further dedicated to providing our customers with the cutting-edge high-quality biomedical/biotechnology/life science research reagents, consumables, and laboratory equipment from Europe and the United States. Our products are CE-certified and ISO-approved. We are able to offer a diverse panels of products due to our European and American manufacturing partners. Our mission is to make health care and research more accessible as quickly as possible.

Our multidisciplinary team is able to provide advisory in a wide range of medical and scientific areas, including preclinical antiviral/non-antiviral drug development, clinical trials, ICH-GCP clinical research monitoring, clinical study reports (CSRs) of pharmaceutical products and clinical evaluation reports (CERs) of medical devices, MDR-GSPRs, and medical device risk analysis.
